Weather apps

Down below we have 4 popular weather apps, 2 of them in English and 2 of them in Dutch (For any students who want to improve their Dutch).

Buienradar

Buienradar is a dutch weather app. It has several options and a great timeline to keep track on the weather.

Apple Android

Darksky

Darksky is a very popular english weather app. This app has some amazing features to keep you updated about the weather.

Apple Android

Buienalarm

Buienalarm is another Dutch weather app. This app is very accuarte and the radar tab has an amazing zoom option.

Apple Android

AccuWeather

AccuWeather is a great app to keep track on the weather. It also has a option to connect with your calendar.

Apple Android
